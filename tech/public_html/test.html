QVC Temple Analytics Challenge

Paul Gregotski and Loi Tran

Web App

Overview

QVC is a video and ecommerce retailer with a live broadcast that reaches nearly 300 million homes worldwide. The data is a 3 month sampling of QVC data that comprises 20% of the actual volume.

There are four questions this Web App seeks to answer:

  1. What are the products and product categories that sell best in the US market?

  2. How effective are QVC’s campaigns in driving product sales?

  3. How effective are QVC’s on-air broadcast and on-air personalities in driving product sales?

  4. What is the impact of QVC’s social networking presence on driving product sales?


QVC Data Analytics Web Application




Select a Question to Interact with the Data.

What are the products and product categories that sell best in the US market?

Data Overview:

Total sales of QVC products in the U.S.A.

  • Hover over the state to check total sales of that state.
  • Click on the state to open a pie chart of that state's product categories.
  • Click on a county in that state to open a pie chart of that countie's product categories.
  • Hover over slices of the pie charts to check specific product percentages.
  • Click on slices of the pie charts to check specific information of that slice.

State Pie Chart

County Pie Chart

How effective are QVC’s campaigns in driving product sales?

Data Overview:

Efficacy of Campaign Spending

  • Click a point to check whether a point is above or below average sales.
  • Hover over a marker to check that specific campaign on that specific category.
  • Toggle a category by selecting it in the legend.
  • Drag the mouse to select an area on the Scatter Plot to zoom in on the graph.
  • Move across the regression line to formulate the efficacy of campaign spending at that point along the x-axis.

How effective are QVC’s on-air broadcast and on-air personalities in driving product sales?

What is the impact of QVC’s social networking presence on driving product sales?

Sentiment Score: The aggregate of all sentiments with assigned values over the given time period, in this case the time period is 12 hours.

  • Strongly Positive = 3 points
  • Positive = 1 point
  • Neutral = 0 points
  • Negative = -1 points
  • Strongly Negative = -3 points
